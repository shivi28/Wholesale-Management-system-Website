WHOLESALE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM

introduction


    Wholesale Management System is developed to improve the efficiency and performance of daily business activity of the wholesaler.


    This project will develop a computerized system for helping the wholesaler in processing the required information and transactions.


functions


Purchase record management:


    This feature enables us to maintain the information of supplier, the products and the quantity supplied.


Stock Management:


    This feature enables us to maintain the information of the products we have and their respective quantities.


Customer and Staff information Management:


    This feature enables to maintain all the information of customers and employees.


Payment information Management:


    This feature enables us to maintain the information of every order and its corresponding bill.
